Careers

At Medipol, every member of our team is working towards better health and brighter futures for people worldwide, and to do that we empower them to shine at every step







With 30,000 colleagues around the world, a strong growth strategy and a reputation built on outstanding values and a history of success, Medipol is a pharmaceutical industry leader. As a member of our team, you will be empowered to build better solutions, be part of world-class research and development, and take on career development opportunities.






Job Select And Apply



You can search for jobs at Medipol by keyword, job number, category or location. And, when you register for job alerts, we will email jobs to you that meet your requirements.






Why Work At Medipol






PEOPLE FIRST
Here, everyone matters and you will be a vital contributor to our inspiring, bold mission. You will help make an impact on people's lives and change futures every day. We are equally committed to bringing out your best and fostering a collaborative workplace built on trust and respect.
OUTSTANDING VALUES
Our culture is rooted in a philosophy we call 'Medipol-ism.' For more than 30 years, we have cultivated an ethical work environment filled with Integrity, Fairness, Honesty and Perseverance. We take actions and make decisions by focusing on patient-centricity. Our values guide how we work and can be felt across every part of our company.




TRUST AND EMPOWERMENT
Our people understand our commitment to therapeutic medical needs. And we trust them to help Medipol reliably deliver innovative, high quality products to patients. It's challenging work, but together we strive every day for the health of people worldwide and the sustained growth of the company.




Empowering Our People to Shine






At Medipol, you will receive support from your team, be inspired by leaders, and have ownership over your career and life. We empower our people to take charge of their potential. We offer programs to teach the future leaders of our company and industry, and training and development programs tailored to help you build valuable skills and succeed in your career. As you discover your potential, your professional and personal future will become brighter every day.
