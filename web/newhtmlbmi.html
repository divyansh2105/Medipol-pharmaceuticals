BMI Calculator







This calculator computes the body mass index and rates it appropriately. This calculator tells how much weight you need to gain or lose in order to achieve your goal and also provides you with the best diet plan. The SBMI - an index that has been newly developed especially for this calculator - serves for this purpose. It is based on the results of the most comprehensive study published so far on the BMI and its associated health risks.
